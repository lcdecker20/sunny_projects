Our Attractions


Here at Sunny Island we have various activities and events to enjoy. With the year round beautiful weather, you're able to adore what Sunny Island brings to the table. Whether it's the breath taking beaches, astonishing amusement parks and humungous malls.

Beaches

The beaches located on Sunny Island not only are beautiful to look at but also full of services, such as restaurants, gift shops and luxury hotels to stay at.

Malls

Sunny Island is known for our worlds biggest mall, Sun Fun Mall. With over 5 floors of a variety of necessities, Sun Fun Mall will be a must see for you and your family.

Amusement Park

With one of the best roller coaster rides in the world, Sunny Island Amusement Park will be sure to bring you excitement. Come on over and have a fun filled day!

Resorts

Sunny Island has some of the world's finest resorts! Each resort is an all-inclusive, providing for a unforgettable island experience! Sunny Island has five different resorts to choose from, each with their own unique theme, including A Tropical Island Getaway, Scuba Shelters, and Hawaiian Hotel. Any resort you stay at will be sure to bring fun to the whole family!
